What, you might ask, is a Pizza Throwdown?

A pizza throwdown is a gathering of all that is pizza under one roof. During this year’s Pizza Throwdown, join over 1,000 other pizza lovers for the opportunity to sample and taste pizza from 20+ of the best pizza makers in Michigan. Each ticket entitles you to 5 complimentary samples from your choice of pizza makers. Each pizza maker will also be selling additional slices (price to be determined by the vendor) and offering all of their best pizza creations for your enjoyment. There will also be a full bar, beer, and non-alcoholic sodas and bottle water for purchase. Entertainment will be provided by DJ Tom T. Don’t forget to also take a look at the 10+ merchants offerings while enjoying your favorite slice.
