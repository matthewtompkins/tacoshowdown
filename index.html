What is a Taco Showdown?

The Taco Showdown is a gathering of all things Tacos under one roof. During this year’s Taco Showdown, join over 1,000 other Taco lovers for the opportunity to sample and taste Tacos (And Salsa) from 15 of the best taquerias in Michigan. Each ticket entitles you to a complimentary taco from your choice of taquerias, as well as a complimentary house margarita.  Each restaurant/chef will also be selling additional tacos (price to be determined by the vendor) and offering all of their creations for your enjoyment. There will also be a full bar, beer, and non-alcoholic sodas and bottle water available for purchase. Entertainment will be provided by one of Detroit’s Top DJs.  Don’t forget to also take a look at the 10+ merchants offerings while enjoying your favorite taco along with some great interactive games to enjoy!
